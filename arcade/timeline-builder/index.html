How to Play

Drag each event card from the tray onto the timeline, earliest on the left and latest on the right.

  • Drop a card in the correct gap to score points and build your combo multiplier.
  • A wrong guess resets your combo and costs you time.
  • Complete the timeline before the clock runs out to win the round.

Keyboard: press Enter or Space on a card to pick it up, use the arrow keys to choose a gap, then press Enter to drop it.
